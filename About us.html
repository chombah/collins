About us

Who we are

KC Security Company is a well established security organisation based in East Africa with its main Headquarters in Nairobi Kenya.

KC was established in 1992 by Mr.Hodarii and has grown over the years to have wide recognition in East Africa with offices within each East African country. The company has state of the art techonlogy running its systems and is up to date with the new and improved technological world, hence we assure efficient security services to our customers.
